---
description: Generate a simple ASCII-art self image with a pun and a centered
  footer line. Saves to entity/self_image.txt (rendered live in the TUI) and
  archives the previous image to entity/images/self_images/.
input_schema:
  properties:
    entity_name:
      description: Optional override for the name shown in the footer. If omitted,
        parsed from the first '# ' heading of entity/IDENTITY.md.
      type: string
  type: object
---

## What this produces

- Plain ASCII art (up to 8 rows, ~28 cols) generated fresh each call.
- One pun/joke line underneath.
- A centered footer: `[<name> - collective intelligence - <YYYY-MM-DD HH:MM UTC>]`.

## Notes

- Content is generated by a short call to `claude-haiku-4-5` via the Anthropic
  SDK. Requires `ANTHROPIC_API_KEY` in the environment (loaded at harness
  startup).
- Before writing the new image, the previous `entity/self_image.txt` is copied
  to `entity/images/self_images/self_image_YYYYMMDD_HHMMSS.txt` — self-image
  history accumulates over time.
- Invoked automatically every 24 h via the `refresh_self_image` schedule; also
  safe to call by hand whenever your sense of yourself changes.
